Hi, I'm Cole Striler. When I was in 9th grade, I founded a business called Xuxa Socks. Within five years, Xuxa Socks was selling over 30,000 pairs of socks a year, which I used to help pay way through UC Berkeley. In college, I learned how to code and did a summer data science internship at NASA. My senior year, I built a web app that that landed me a full-time job working at Apple on the online store as a backend developer.

In my free time, I love motorcycle riding, chess, music, jiu-jitsu, rock climbing, surfing, and startups.
